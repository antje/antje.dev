As a Member of Technical Staff at Amazon AGI Labs, I drive how developers discover, learn, and build with Amazon's next-generation AI. I work cross-functionally with Research, Product, and Engineering to translate emerging model capabilities into practical frameworks and guidance that help teams ship real applications. Online Course
